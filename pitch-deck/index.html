Datistica 02

Most AI tells you what will happen. Almost none tells you what to do about it.

We believe the next decade of value won't come from bigger predictions — it will come from turning prediction into the right decision, under real constraints, real risk, and real cost.

We're building the lab that owns that layer in Latin America.

Datistica 03

The problem

Prediction got cheap.
Good decisions didn't.

  • The forecast is no longer the bottleneck — the decision that follows it is.
  • Value leaks when humans guess the action under constraints the model never saw.
  • In LATAM, high-stakes decisions still run on informal heuristics — bias-prone. [18]
  • Deep technical talent is underused or leaving — PhD underemployment, brain drain. [18]
