The gap we close

Most AI tells you what might happen. We build systems that tell you what to do.

A good forecast is not the same as a good decision. You can predict demand accurately and still run out of stock. You can flag equipment at risk and still build a plan your team cannot execute. The model works. The outcome does not.

Datistica builds the full path from data to action. We research how prediction and decision-making fit together, then turn that research into products companies can deploy.

What we research

  • Learning for decisions Models trained on whether the outcome works, not just whether the prediction is close
  • From prediction to action Systems that connect forecasting directly to the choices your operation needs to make
  • Decisions under uncertainty Methods for allocating resources when the future is unclear
  • Domain-specific models Small, focused models built for how your industry actually operates

What we ship

Forecasting engines

Models built for your domain — demand, production, pricing, equipment health. Designed to feed directly into the decisions that follow, not to sit in a dashboard.

Optimization layers
