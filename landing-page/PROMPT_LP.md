Build a single-page landing site for "Datistica", an applied AI research lab
based in Rio de Janeiro, Brazil, focused on decision intelligence.

AESTHETIC — strictly minimal, in the style of thinkingmachines.ai:
- Pure white background (#FFFFFF), near-black text (#111111), one single accent
  color used sparingly (deep blue, #1A3C6E) for links and small details only.
- Typography-led: a classic serif for headlines (Georgia or a Garamond-like
  webfont) and a clean sans-serif or monospace for body/nav. Large, confident
  type. Generous whitespace. Max content width ~720px, centered.
- NO stock photos, NO gradients, NO drop-shadow cards, NO icon grids. At most
  one abstract element near the top: a small generative/geometric SVG motif —
  a minimal network of nodes converging into a single decision point.
- Subtle only: 1px hairline dividers (#E5E5E5), understated hovers, slow
  fade-in on scroll. No parallax. Fully responsive, fast, semantic HTML.

STRUCTURE (single page, anchor nav):
1. Top nav: "Datistica" wordmark left; links right: Research, Approach,
   Sectors, Contact. PT/EN toggle (visual only is fine).
2. Hero: wordmark large, then the manifesto sentence:
   "We build decision systems — models that don't just predict what will
   happen, but prescribe what to do about it."
   Then one short paragraph: an applied research lab combining machine
   learning, simulation, and mathematical optimization, with a research focus
   on Decision-Focused Learning and Predict-then-Optimize methods, founded in
   Latin America to bring state-of-the-art decision science to real-world
   resource allocation.
3. "Our thesis" — essay-style, 2–3 short paragraphs, no bullets: prediction
   alone is not a decision; the gap between forecasting and acting is where
   value is lost; we train predictive models against the quality of the
   decisions they induce.
4. "What we build" — three text blocks split by hairlines (not cards):
   Prediction (ML/DL, time series, simulation, specialized small models and
   agents), Prescription (linear and mixed-integer optimization under
   uncertainty), Integration (Decision-Focused Learning, contextual stochastic
   optimization, end-to-end decision pipelines).
5. "Sectors" — one elegant line, not logos: Energy & O&G · Industry ·
   Finance · Healthcare.
6. "Principles" — three one-line commitments in serif italic: rigorous and
   published methods; responsible and transparent AI; real-world impact over
   hype.
7. Footer: minimal — Datistica, Rio de Janeiro — Brazil, a contact email,
   LinkedIn, © 2026.

TONE: sober, scientific, confident, zero buzzwords ("revolutionary",
"cutting-edge", "unlock" are banned). All copy in English with the quiet
authority of a research lab, not a consultancy.
